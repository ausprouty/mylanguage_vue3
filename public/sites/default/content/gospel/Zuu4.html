2. Umuntu WONILE, futhi UHLUKENE noNkulunkulu ngakho akalwazi uthando lukaNkulunkulu nepulani lakhe ngokuphila kwakhe.

UMUNTU WONILE

“Ngokuba bonke bonile, basilalewe inkazimulo kaNkulunkulu”. Roma 3:23

Umuntu wayedalelwe ukuba abe nobuhlobo noNkulunkulu, kodwa umuntu wazikhethela eyakhe indlela ngakho lobo buhlobo balimala. Lokhu iBhayibheli likubiza ngokuthi isoni, kubonakaliswa ukumelana noNkulunkulu, nokungabinandaba noNkulunkulu, nangokungaphili impilo ephelele ayibekelwe nguNkulunkulu.

UMUNTU UHLUKENE NONKULUNKULU

“Ngokuba inkokhelo yesono ingukufa” [ukwahlukana komoya noNkulunkulu] Roma 6:23

UNkulunkulu uNgcwele. Umuntu uyisoni. Kukhona isikhulu phakathi kwabo. Imicibisholo ikhombisa imizamo yomuntu ukufinyelela kuNkulunkulu nasempilweni echichimayo ngokuphila impilo elungile, nangokusonta, nangokusiza abanye, nokunye, kodwa imizamo yonke ayimfikisi Ndawo.

Lokhu okulandelayo kuchaza ukuthi ungamamukela kanjani uKrisu:

UNGAMAMUKELA UKRISTU KHONA MANJE

(Ukukhuleka ukukhuluma noNkulunkulu)

Mcele ukuba angene empilweni yakho. Methembe ukuthi uzokufeza lokho akuthembisileyo. Yena unaka kakhulu okufiswa yinhliziyo yakho kunokushiwo ngamawzi omlomo wakho. Ungakhuleka ngalendlela elandelayo:

“Nkosi Jesu, ngiyakudinga. Kade ngiziphathele mina ukuphila kwami ngalokho ngonile kuwe. Ngiyabonga ukuthi ungithethelele izono zami. Ngena empilweni yami uyibuse. Ngenze ngibe yilokho othanda ukuba ngibeyikhona”.

Ngabe lomthandazo usho okufiswa yinhliziyo yakho na?

Uma usho khona, thandaza manje, khona uKristu ezongena empilweni yakho njengokuthembisa kwakhe.

UNGAZI KANJANI UKUTHI UKRISTU USESEMPILWENI YAKHO?

Usumamukele yini uKristu empilweni yakho? Ngokwesithembiso sakhe esitholakala ku Isambulo 3:20, uphi uKristu manje na? UKristu uthembise ukuthi uNkulunkulu usewuphendulile umthandzo wakho? (Ngokwithemba uNkulunkulu neZwi lakhe).

IBHAYIBELI LITHEMBISA UKUPHILA OKUPHAKADE KUBO BONKE ABAMUKELA UKRISTU

“UNkulunkulu usipha ukuphila okuphakade, nalokhukuphila kuseNdodaneni yakhe. Onayo iNdodana kaNkulunkulu ayunakho ukuphila. Nginilobele lokho, ukuze nazi ukuthi nikuphila okuphakade nina enikholwa egameni leNdodana kaNKulunkulu”. (1 Johane 5:11-13)

Mbonge njalo uNkulunkulu ngoba uKristu usesempilweni yakho. Ushilo ukuthi akasoze akushiye (KumaHeberu 13:5). Usungazi ukuthi uKristu uphila ngaphakathi kuwena nokuthi unokuphila okuphakade ngenxa yesethembiso sakhe.

Isikhumbuzo esibalulekile…

UNGAHAMBI NGEMIZWA

Isithembiso sezwi likaNkulunkulu siyigunya lethu, hayi imizwa yethu. Ikholwa liphila ngokukholwa (ngokwethemba) ekwethembekeni kuka Nkulunkulu nezwi lakhe, imizwa (umvuso wokukholwa kwethu noku lalela) (Funda u Johane 14:21).

Lesitimela sizohamba nendlu yomshayeli ngaphandle kwendlu yomshayeli. Kungeke kwasiza ukuzama ukudonsa isitimela ngendlu yomshayeli. Njengamakholwa asithembeli.Emizweni kodwa sibeka ukukholwa kwethu (ithemba) ekwethembekeni kuka Nkulunkulu nasezithembisweni zeZwi lakhe.

MANJE USUMAMUKELE UKRISTU

    Ngesikhathi owamukele ngaso uKristu kwenzeke izinto eziningi kuwena njengalezi:
    UKristu ungenile empilweni yakho (Isambulo 3:20; KwabaseKolose 1:27).
    Izono zakho zithethelelwe (KwabaseKolose 1:14)
    Usungumntwana kaNkulunkulu (Johane 1:12)
    Wamukele ukuphila okuphakade (Johane 5:24)
    Usuzoqala ukulazi ipulani likaNkulunkulu nenjongo yakhe ngempilo yakho (Johane 10:10; II KwabaseKorinte 5:14, 15, 17)

Ikhona kodwa into enkulu ngaphezu kwalena osuyenzile yokwamukela uKristu? Ungathanda yini ukubonga uNkulunkulu ngomthandazo manje ngalokho akwenzele khona? Ukumbonga uNkulunkulu kubonisa ukukholwa, Masithandaze.

UNGAKHULA KANJANI ENKOSINI

Kudingekile ukuba ukhule ekukholweni kwakho. Lokhu okulandelayo kuzokusiza ukuba ukhule.

    Khuleka kuNkulunkulu njalo (Johane 15:7).
    Funda iZwi likaNkulunkulu njalo (Izenzo 17:11) qale ngo Johane.
    Lalela uNkulunkulu njalo (Johane 14:21).
    Fakazela uKristu ngempilo nangamazwi akho (Matewu 4:19; Johane 15:8).
    Ncika kuNkulunkulu kuyo yonke into eqondene nokuphila kwakho (I Petru 5:7).
    Vumela uMoya oNgcwele ukuba akubuse, akuphe amandla yonke imihla ukuze ube-ngufakazi kaKristu (KwabaseGalathiya 5:16,17; Izenzo 1:8).

IBANDLA LIBALULEKILE

Izinkuni zivutha kahle uma zihlanganisiwe; kodwa uma ukhipha olunye luzovutha, ulubeke eceleni luyacima. Kanjalo nawe angeke uyiphile kahle impilo yokukholwa uma ungazihlanganisi namanye amakholwa.

Kubalulekile ukuba uhambe uyozihlanganisa namanye abamukele uJesu Kristu. Kufanele uye ebandleni elinabantu abakholwayo ukuthi iBhayibheli liqinisile, futhi lapho uJesu Kristu ehlonishwa futhi elalelwa khona. (KumaHeberu 10:25)
