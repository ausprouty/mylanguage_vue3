(Amanqaku akule ncwadana makafundwe ngokwayamene neBhayibhile ngalo lonke ixesha xa kunokwenzeka).

2. Umntu WONILE waza WOHLUKANA kuThixo, ngoko ke akanalo ulwazi namava othando lukaThixo kwakunye necebo lakhe ngobomi bakhe.

UMNTU WON I LE

“Kuba bonile bonke, basilela uluzukweni lukaThixo.” (Roma 3:23)

Umntu wadalwa ukuze abe nobudlelwana noThixo, kodwa ngenxa yentando yakhe elukhuni, wazanyulela ukuhamba indlela yakhe engaxhomekekanga mntwini, baza ke ngoko ubudlelwana phakathi kwakhe noThixo bophuka. Le ntando yakhe, ecaciswa ngumkhwa wobundlobongela okanye ikratshi lokuziphakamisa, bubungqina bento ethi iBhayibhile ukuyibiza sisono.

UMNTU WAHLUKANISIWE

“Kuba umvuzo wesono kukufa.” [ukohlulwa ngokomoya kuThixo] (KwabaseRoma 6:23)

UThixo ungcwele waye umntu ungumoni Kukho umsantsa omkhulu owahlule aba babini. Umntu uzama njalo ukufikelela kuThixo nokuthi afumane ubomi obugqithiseleyo ngemizamo yakhe; ubomi obulungileyo, intlalo entle, ulwazi lobulumko, okanyengenkolo - kodwa onke lamalinge akanamsebenzi.

Inyaniso yesithathu yiyo kuuphela esinika impendulo kule meko…

3. UYesu Kristu nguye KUUPHELA omiswe nguThixo ukuba aqondane nesono somntu. Ngenxa yakhe unganolwazi namava othando lukathixo, necebo Lakhe ngobomi bakho.

WAFA ENDAWENI YETHU

“Ke yena uThixo uqondakalisa okwakhe ukusithanda ngokuthi qthi sisengaboni, uKristu asifele.” (KwabaseRoma 5:8)

WAVUKA KWABAFILEYO

"UKristu wafela izono zethu…Wangcwatywa… Wavuka ekufeni ngomhla wesithathu, ngokweziBhalo… Wabonakala kuPetros, wabonwa ngabo bonke abathunywa abalushumi elinambini. Emva koko ubonakele ngaxeshanye nakubalandeli bakhe abangaphezu kwamakhulu amahlanu…" (1kwabaseKorinte 15:3-6)

NGUYE KUUPHELA INDLELA

"Uthi uYesu kuye, 'Ndim indela, ndim inyaniso, ndim ubomi, akukho namnye uzayo kuBawo, engezi ngam.'” (Yohane 14:6)

UThixo uwuphelisile umsantsa osahluleyo kuYe ngokuthumela uNyana wakhe, uYesu Kristu, ukuba afe emnqamlezweni endaweni yethu.

Akwanele ukuzazi nje ezinyaniso zintathu…

4. Sonke-ngabmnye ngamnye ma SIMAMKELE uYesu uKristu njengomsindisi neNkosi, kukhona siyakuba nolwazi namava othando lukaThixo necebo ngobomi bethu.

SIMELE UKUMAKELA UKRISTU

“Ke bonke abamamkelayo wabanika igunya lokuba babe ngabantwana bakaThixo, abo ke bakholwayo kwigama lakhe.” (Yohane 1:12)

SAMKELA UKRISTU NGOKHOLO

"Kuba nisindiswe ngokubabalwa ngalo ukholo, nako oko akuphumi kuni, kusipho sikaThixoakuphumi misebenzini, hleze kubekho bani uqhayisayo (Kwabase-ephese 2:8,9)

SAMKELA NGOKUMEMA THINA ZIQU

[UKristu uyathetha] “Yabona, ndimi emnyango ndinkqonkqoza; ukuba uthe umntu waliva izwi lam, waluvula ucango, ndongena kuye, ndidle naye” (Izityhilelo 3:20).

Ukwamkela uKristu kuthetha ukuguqukela kuThixo, ushiye ubuwena, ukuthemba uKristu ukuba uza kungena ebomini bethu, ukuxolela izono nokuthi asenze loo nto afuna ukuba sibe yiyo. Akwanele ukuvuma nje ngengqondo ukuba uYesu Kristu ungunyana ka Thixo nokuba wafa emnqamlezweni efela izono zethu.

Ezi zangqa zibini zimele iintlobo ezimbini zobomi.

Sisiphi na isangqa ongathanda ukuba simele ubomi bakho?

Oku kulandelayo kucacisa indlela ongamamkela ngayo uKristu:

UNGAMAMKELA UKRISTU NGOKU NGOKHOLO OLUBONAKALISWE EMTHANDAZWENI

(Umthandazo kukuthetha noThixo)

UThixo uyayazi intliziyo yakho uhlupheka bubumebentliziyo yakho nangaphezu kokuba ehluphekangamazwi akho. Lo ngumthandazo ongawenza:

“Nkosi Yesu, ndiya phawula ukuba bendikade ndizilawulela ubomi bam ngenxa yoko ndikonile.Ngoku ndivula ucango lobomi bam, ndiya kwamkela njengoMsindisi neNkosi yam. Lawula etronini yabomi bam. Ndenze ukuba ndibelolohlobo lomntu ofuna ukuba ndibe lulo.”

Ingaba lo mthandazo luluvo lomnqweno wentliziyo yakho?

Ukuba ngaba kunjalo, yenza lo mthandazo ngoku, uKristu uza kungena ebomini bakho njengokuthembisaKwakhe.

INDLELA ONOKUTHI WAZI NGAYO UKUBA UKRISTU UKHO EBOMINI BAKHO

Umamkele na uKristu ebomini bakho? Ngokwesithembiso sakhe kwisiTyhilelo 3:20, uphi uKristu kanye ngoku mayelana nawe? UKristu wathi uyakuza ebomini bakho. Ebengakukhohlisa na? Wazi ngaliphi na igunya ukuba uThixo uwuphendule umthandazo wakho? (Ukuthembeka kukaThixo ngokwakhe kwa kunye nelizwi lakhe).

IBHAYIBHILE ITHEMBISA UBOMI OBUNGUNAPHAKADE KUBO BONKE ABAMAMKELAYO UKRISTU

“Bubo obu ke ubungqina; ukuba uThixo usinike ubomi obungunaphakade; baye obo bomi bukuyeuNyana wakhe. Lowo unaye uNyana unabo ubomi, lowo ungenaye unyana kaThixo, akanabo ubomi. Ndinibhalela ezi zinto, nina nikholwayo kwigama loNyana kaThixo, ukuze nazi ukuba ninobomi obungunaphakade.” (I Yohane 5:11-13)

Bulela uThixo ngawo onke amaxesha kuba uKristu echo ebomini bakho waye engasoze akushiye (KumaHebere 13:5). Usenakho ukwazi ukuba uKristu ophilayo uhleli kuwe, nokuba unobomi obungunaphakade, ukusukela nje kwixesha othe wammema ngalo ngokwesithembiso sakhe. Akasoze akukhohlise.

MUSA UKUXHOMEKEKA KWIZIMVO

Sinegunya elisisithembiso sikaThixo, ingasizizo izimvo zethu. UmKristu uphila ngokholo (ithemba) ekuthembekeni kuka Thixo ngokwakhe kanye neLizwi lakhe. Lo mfanekiso kaloliwe ubonisa ubuhlobo phakathi kweyona nto enyanisekileyo (UThixo neLizwi lakhe), kunye nokholo (ithemba lethu kuThixo neLizwi lakhe), kwa kunye noluvo (isiphumo sokholo lwethu nentobeko) (Yohane 14:21)

Uloliwe unakho ukubaleka nokuba likhona, nokuba alikho itroko langasemva. Noxa kunjalo ingabubuyathaukwenza ilinge lokutsala uloliwe ngetroko langasemva. Ngokunjalo, thina njengamaKristu asixhomekeki kwizimvo okanye kwiintshukumo kodwa sibeka ukholo lwethu (ithemba) ekuthembekeni kukaThixo nakwizithembiso zeLizwi lakhe.

KE KALOKU NJENGOKO UMAMKELE UKRISTU

Ngelo xesha kanye lokwamkela kwakho uKristu, nje ngesenzo sokholo, kwenzeke izinto ezininzi ezibandakanya ezi zilandelayo:

    UKristu uzile ebomini bakho. (KwisiTyhilelo 3:20;Kolose 1:27)
    Izono zakho zixolelwe (Kolose 1:14)
    Ungumntwana kaThixo (Yohane 1:12)
    Uzuze ubomi obungunaphakade (Johane 5:24)
    Waza waqala ukuhlahla indlela malunga naloo msebenzi uThixo akudalele wona (Yohane 10:10; 2 AmaKorinte 5:14,15,17; 1 Thesalonika 5:18)

Ungacinga na ukuba ikhona into engummangaliso ekwehleleyo ngaphezu kokwamkela uKristu? Ungathandaza khona ngoku, ngako konke akwenzele kona? Kanye esosenzo sokubulela uThixo salatha ukholo.

Ukonwabela ubomi bakho obutsha ngokupheleleyo…

AMACEBISO NGOKUKHULA KOMKRISTU

Ukuthemba uYesu Kristu kusisiqhamo sokukhula ngokwasemoyeni. “Lowo ulilungisa uya kudla ubomi obuphuma elukholweni.” (Kuma-Galati 3:11) Ubomi bokholo buya kukwenza ukuba uthembe uThixo ngokugqithisileyo ngazo zonke iimeko zobomi bakho; nokuba wenze njalo oku kulandelayo:

    Yiya Yiya kuThixo ngomthandazo yonke imihla. (Yohane 15:7)
    Funda Funda iLizwi likaThixo yonke imihla (Izenzo 17:11) - uqale kwivangeli kaYohane.
    Lulama Lulamela uThixo amaxesha ngamaxesha. (Yohane 14:21)
    Nqhina Nyqinela uKristu ngobomi bakho nangamazwi akho. (Mateyu 4:19; Yohane 15:8)
    Mthembe Waphose kuThixo onke amaxhala akho. (1 Petros 5:7)
    Lawuleka UMoya oyiNgcwele - mvumele alawule, ongeze amandla kubomi bakho bemihla ngemihla nobungqina bakho. (AmaGalati 5:16,17; Izenzo 1:8)

UKUBALULEKA KOBUDLELWANA BOBUKRISTU

Kuma-Hebere 10:25, siyacetyiswa ukuba “singakuhilizeli ukuhlanganisana kwethu…kodwa ma sivuselelane…” linkuni ezininzi zivutha ngokucacileyo, kodwa xa ubeke Iwalunye kwiziko elibandayo, uyacima umlilo. Kunjalo ukuhlobana kwakho namanyeamaKristu. Ukuba awuyi enkonzweni apho uKristu awongwa khona, nalapho iLizwi lakhe lishunyayelwa khona thabatha amanyathelo, uzibandakanye njalo nabantu esebemfumene uKristu njengoMsindisi, benamava othando Iwakhe.
